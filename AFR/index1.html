ABOUT US

Axlr8r Formula Racing is a Formula SAE team of IIT, Delhi. The team comprises of around 25 undergraduate students from various departments who fabricate an open wheel electric formula race car. We innovate, design and excel!.

COMPETITION SEASON 2020

In the competition season 2019, Axlr8r Formula Racing competed in two prestigious Formula Student competitions – in the Formula Bharat and in Formula Student Germany.

FORMULA STUDENT GERMANY

Our team became the first and the only Indian Electric team to clear all technical inspections approving all German standards and the first and the only Indian electric team to drive our car on German tracks

FORMULA STUDENT BHARAT

Axlr8r Formula Racing won the overall Runners Up trophy in the event.
